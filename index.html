The Rules

Objective:

  • Get a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer, without going bust (over 21)

Card Values:

  • 2-10: face value
  • Royals: 10 points
  • Aces: 1 or 11 points, depending on which value benefits the hand more

Gameplay:

  • Dealer is dealt two cards, one hidden, one visible
  • Player is dealt two cards, both visible
  • Player chooses to "Hit" (take a card) or "Stand" (keep current hand) to get as close to, but not over, 21
  • If the player busts, the round is immediately over and the dealer wins
  • When the player clicks "Stand", the dealer draws until their hand value is 17 or higher
  • Both hands are compared - the closest to 21, without going bust, wins
